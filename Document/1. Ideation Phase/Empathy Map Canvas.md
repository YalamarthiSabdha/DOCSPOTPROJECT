# Empathy Map Canvas
## Project: DOC-APPOINTMENT – Doctor Appointment Booking System

---

## Empathy Map: PATIENT (User)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    WHO ARE WE EMPATHIZING WITH?                 │
│              Patient / User looking to book a doctor            │
│                    Goal: Book an appointment fast               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

### SAYS
> "I don't know which doctor is available right now."
> "I called the clinic 5 times and no one answered."
> "I wish I could check the doctor's fees before visiting."
> "I want to know if my appointment is confirmed."
> "I had a great doctor last time but I can't remember his number."

---

### THINKS
- "Is this doctor qualified enough?"
- "Will they actually be available at the given time?"
- "I hope I don't have to wait for hours."
- "I want to see doctor reviews before booking."
- "What if I need to cancel or reschedule?"

---

### DOES
- Searches online for nearby doctors
- Calls clinics and hospitals for appointments
- Physically visits clinics to check availability
- Asks friends and family for doctor recommendations
- Waits in long queues at hospitals

---

### FEELS
- **Frustrated** by long waiting times and lack of information
- **Anxious** about medical emergencies with no quick access to doctors
- **Confused** about which specialist to consult for what condition
- **Relieved** when they find a trusted, verified doctor quickly
- **Grateful** for a platform that simplifies the entire process

---

## Empathy Map: DOCTOR

### SAYS
> "Managing appointments manually is very difficult."
> "Patients often forget their appointments and don't show up."
> "I want patients to rate my service."
> "I need software to track my daily schedule."

### THINKS
- "I want my profile to look professional online."
- "I wish I could get notified instantly when someone books."
- "Managing paper records is too time-consuming."

### DOES
- Maintains a physical register of appointments
- Calls patients to remind them of appointments
- Manually manages timings and availability
- Submits certificates for verification

### FEELS
- **Overwhelmed** by manual administrative work
- **Professional** when using a modern digital platform
- **Satisfied** when patients leave positive reviews

---

## Empathy Map: ADMIN

### SAYS
> "I need to verify doctor credentials before they are listed."
> "I need to manage all users and doctors from one place."
> "I want to block fake doctor profiles immediately."

### THINKS
- "Is this doctor's certificate valid?"
- "Are all users genuinely registered?"
- "I need real-time visibility into platform activity."

### DOES
- Reviews and approves doctor applications
- Manages user accounts
- Monitors appointment activity
- Blocks/unblocks users as needed

### FEELS
- **Responsible** for platform quality and safety
- **In control** with a centralized dashboard
- **Confident** when all data is organized and accessible

---

## Key Insights from Empathy Mapping

| Insight | Action Taken in Project |
|---|---|
| Patients need trusted, verified doctors | Admin approval + MBBS certificate upload |
| Doctors need schedule management | Doctor appointments dashboard |
| Patients want feedback mechanism | Rating & review after completed appointments |
| All roles need real-time updates | Notification system for key events |
| Admin needs oversight | Admin panel with user/doctor management |
